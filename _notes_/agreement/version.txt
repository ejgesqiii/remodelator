CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into by and between Startwave Technology Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Consultant"), and ________________ (the "Company"), effective as of March 1, 2026 (the "Effective Date").

1. Consulting Relationship and Services

● The Consultant's relationship with the Company is that of an independent contractor, and not an employee.
● The Consultant shall be solely responsible for the method, details, and means of performing the Services.
● Services (the "Services"): The Consultant shall provide software development services for Remodelator v5.0, a contractor estimating platform, as detailed in the Remodelator v5.0 Proposal Outline. Services include:
  ○ Full-stack development (React/TypeScript frontend, Python/FastAPI backend)
  ○ Database design and implementation (Google Cloud Firestore)
  ○ Authentication and authorization system
  ○ Core estimate management features
  ○ Item catalog and tree navigation
  ○ Real-time pricing with LLM integration
  ○ Payment processing and subscription management (Stripe)
  ○ Admin portal and user activity tracking
  ○ Deployment and hosting configuration
  ○ Data migration from legacy system

2. Term and Termination

● Term: The Consultant shall serve commencing on the Effective Date and terminating upon completion and delivery of Remodelator v5.0, estimated 6-8 weeks. Upon completion of v5.0, the Company shall have the option to continue the Consultant's services for stretch features, sales support, and marketing activities under terms to be mutually agreed in writing.
● Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time upon ten (10) business days' written notice.
● Survival: Sections 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 shall survive termination or expiration of this Agreement.

3. Compensation and Expenses

● Fees: As consideration for the Services, the Company shall pay the Consultant as follows:
  ○ Milestone 1: $3,000 upon completion of working application prototype including authentication, navigation, initial API layer, and initial data layer setup.
  ○ Milestone 2: $7,000 upon completion and delivery of Remodelator v5.0 with all core features, payments, admin portal, deployment, and data migration.
● Total Compensation: $10,000
● Expenses: The Company shall reimburse the Consultant for reasonable development expenses outlined in the Remodelator v5.0 Proposal, including:
  ○ AI development tools (Cursor/Claude Sonnet): estimated $600-$1,000
  ○ Cloud hosting and infrastructure costs during development and initial operations
  ○ Domain registration and SSL certificates
  ○ Third-party API costs (LLM providers, Stripe testing, SendGrid)
● Expense Approval: Expenses exceeding amounts specified in the Proposal require prior written approval from the Company's authorized representative.

4. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property

● Confidential Information: The Consultant acknowledges that during the engagement, the Company may disclose business information, customer data, and proprietary information. The Consultant shall maintain such information in strict confidence and use it solely for the benefit of the Company in performing the Services.
● IP Ownership: All code, documentation, designs, and other work product created by the Consultant in connection with the Services (the "Work Product") shall be the exclusive property of the Company. The Consultant hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in the Work Product to the Company, including all intellectual property rights.
● Company Property Return: At the time of termination, the Consultant will return to the Company all devices, records, data, or other property belonging to the Company, and will provide all source code, documentation, and credentials necessary for the Company to maintain and operate the application.

5. Independent Contractor Status and Taxes

● The Consultant shall have full responsibility for all applicable taxes for all compensation paid under this Agreement.
● The Consultant agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold the Company harmless from any liability or claims with respect to such taxes or employment requirements.

6. Non-Solicitation

● During the term of the Relationship, and for a period of twelve (12) months immediately following the termination of the Relationship, the Consultant shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit any of the Company's employees, consultants, or contractors to terminate their relationship with the Company.

● Indemnification: Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other party from and against all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from any negligent or wrongful act or omission, or breach of any representation or warranty in this Agreement by the indemnifying party.
● Limitation of Liability: In no event shall either party be liable for any indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.
