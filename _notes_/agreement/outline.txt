CONSULTING AGREEMENT OUTLINE
Essential Components for One-Page Agreement Between Companies

• HEADER & PARTIES
  - Agreement title
  - Company names and legal entities (e.g., "Delaware corporation")
  - Effective date

• CONSULTING RELATIONSHIP & SERVICES
  - Independent contractor status (not employee)
  - Description of services to be provided
  - Consultant responsible for method/means of performing services

• TERM & TERMINATION
  - Start date and end conditions
  - Termination for convenience clause (notice period)
  - Survival clause (which sections remain valid after termination)

• COMPENSATION & EXPENSES
  - Payment structure (hourly, commission, fixed fee, etc.)
  - Payment timing and limitations
  - Expense responsibility and approval process

• CONFIDENTIALITY & INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
  - Scope of confidential information (if limited or not applicable)
  - IP ownership/assignment (if applicable to services)
  - Return of company property at termination

• INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS & TAXES
  - Tax responsibility (consultant handles own taxes)
  - Indemnification for tax/employment issues

• NON-SOLICITATION
  - Protection against soliciting employees/consultants
  - Duration (e.g., 12 months after termination)

• INDEMNIFICATION & LIABILITY
  - Mutual indemnification for negligence/breach
  - Limitation of liability caps
  - Exclusion of consequential damages

• GOVERNING LAW & ENTIRE AGREEMENT
  - Choice of law (state/jurisdiction)
  - Entire agreement clause (supersedes prior discussions)

• SIGNATURE BLOCKS
  - Company name, authorized signatory, title
  - Consultant name, authorized signatory, title
  - Addresses for notices
