REMODELATOR v5.0 - CLIENT PROPOSAL OUTLINE
============================================

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

   Remodelator v5.0 is a complete rebuild of the contractor estimating platform using 
   modern cloud-native architecture. This proposal outlines the development of a SaaS 
   application that enables remodeling contractors to create detailed project estimates, 
   manage item catalogs, generate customer proposals, and process payments.

   KEY IMPROVEMENTS FROM LEGACY SYSTEM:
   - Modern tech stack 
   - LLM-powered real-time pricing
   - Subscription billing with Stripe
   - Responsive design
   - Cloud hosting with minimal operational costs
   - Enhanced UX with improved workflows

   THIS PROPOSAL INCLUDES:
   - Complete feature inventory (core + new + admin)
   - Technical architecture and technology stack
   - Scope of work breakdown
   - Development cost estimates ($10,000 labor + $600-1,000 AI tools)
   - Ongoing hosting costs ($35-125/month)
   - 4 completion-based payment milestones
   - Sales & marketing considerations
   - Stretch features for future expansion
   - Recommendations on sales and marketing

   TOTAL INVESTMENT: $10,000-11,000 (development) + monthly hosting

       - Cloud Storage (files/PDFs): $0-2/month
       - Domain: $1/month ($12/year)

       Total Monthly Hosting (Low Usage):

       Total Monthly Hosting (High Usage - 50+ active users):

       Cost Per User Economics:
       - At 10 users: $7.50-12.50 per user/month
