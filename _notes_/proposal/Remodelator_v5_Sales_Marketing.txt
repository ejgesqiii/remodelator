REMODELATOR v5.0 - SALES & MARKETING
=====================================

Go-to-market strategy and customer acquisition.

---

1. TRADE SHOWS
   - Target shows: NAHB (National Association of Home Builders), regional contractor expos
   - Booth setup and materials
   - Demo laptops/tablets
   - Collateral (flyers, business cards, swag)
   - Lead capture system (scan badges, QR codes)
   
   COSTS PER SHOW:
   - Booth space: $2K-10K
   - Travel/lodging: $1K-3K
   - Materials: $500-2K
   - Staff time: 3-5 days
   
   ESTIMATE:
   - 2-4 shows per year
   - Total: $15K-50K/year

---

2. ADS
   - Google Ads (search: "contractor estimating software")
   - Facebook/Instagram (target contractors, remodelers)
   - LinkedIn Ads (target construction professionals)
   - YouTube pre-roll (how-to contractor content)
   - Retargeting campaigns
   
   BUDGET:
   - Testing phase: $1K-2K/month
   - Scale phase: $5K-10K/month
   - Target CPA: $50-150 per signup
   
   CREATIVE NEEDS:
   - Landing pages (A/B testing)
   - Ad copy variations
   - Video demos (30-60 sec)

---

3. OUTBOUND
   - Cold email campaigns to contractor lists
   - LinkedIn outreach
   - Cold calling (if budget allows)
   - Partner with trade organizations
   
   TOOLS:
   - Email finder (Apollo, Hunter)
   - Email automation (Instantly, Lemlist)
   - CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive)
   
   VOLUME:
   - 500-1000 emails per week
   - 5-10% reply rate target
   - 1-2% conversion to demo
   
   LABOR:
   - SDR (Sales Dev Rep): $50K-70K/year
   - Or outsource: $2K-5K/month

---

4. REPLY MANAGEMENT
   - Respond to inbound inquiries within 1 hour
   - Qualify leads (budget, timeline, fit)
   - Book demos
   - Follow-up sequences
   - Nurture cold leads
   
   CHANNELS:
   - Email
   - Website chat (Intercom, Drift)
   - Phone
   - Social media DMs
   
   LABOR:
   - Part-time SDR: $3K-5K/month
   - Full-time SDR: $50K-70K/year
   - Or founder handles initially

---

5. DEMOS
   - Live product demos (Zoom/Google Meet)
   - Pre-recorded demo videos
   - Self-serve trial period
   - Onboarding walkthrough
   
   DEMO PROCESS:
   - Discovery call (15 min) - understand needs
   - Live demo (30 min) - show relevant features
   - Q&A (15 min)
   - Follow-up email with trial link
   
   CONVERSION:
   - Demo-to-trial: 50-70%
   - Trial-to-paid: 20-40%
   - Total demo-to-paid: 10-28%
   
   LABOR:
   - Founder/sales rep conducts demos
   - 5-10 demos per week at scale

---

FUNNEL MATH

Assumptions (monthly):
- Website visitors: 1,000
- Visitor-to-signup: 5% = 50 signups
- Signups-to-demo request: 30% = 15 demos
- Demo-to-trial: 60% = 9 trials
- Trial-to-paid: 30% = 3 customers

Cost per customer: $300-500 (blended CAC)

---

SALES TEAM HIRING PLAN

Phase 1 (Months 1-6): Founder-led sales
- Founder does everything
- Learn what messaging works
- Refine demo script

Phase 2 (Months 6-12): Add part-time SDR
- SDR handles replies and books demos
- Founder closes deals
- Cost: $3K-5K/month

Phase 3 (Year 2+): Full sales team
- Full-time SDR: $50K-70K
- Account Executive (closer): $70K-100K + commission
- Marketing Manager: $60K-90K

---

KEY METRICS TO TRACK

- Website traffic
- Signup conversion rate
- Demo request rate
- Demo show rate
- Demo-to-trial rate
- Trial-to-paid rate
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV:CAC ratio (target 3:1)
