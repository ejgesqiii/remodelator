STARTWAVE TECHNOLOGY INC.
REFERRAL AND CONSULTING AGREEMENT
This Referral and Consulting Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into by and between
Startwave Technology Inc., a Delaware corporation (the “Company”), and ________________
(the “Consultant”), effective as of ________________ (the “Effective Date”).
1. Consulting Relationship and Services
● The Consultant’s relationship with the Company is that of an independent contractor,
and not an employee.
● The Consultant shall be solely responsible for the method, details, and means of
performing the Services.
● Services (the "Services"): The Consultant shall provide the following services:
○ Introductions to potential clients.
○ Feedback on the Company's platform.
2. Term and Termination
● Term: The Consultant shall serve as a consultant commencing on Effective Date and
terminating on the earlier of (a) the date the Consultant completes the provision of the
Services, or (b) the date the Consultant is paid the maximum consulting fees specified in
Section 3.
● Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time
upon ten (10) business days’ written notice.
● Survival: Sections 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 shall survive termination or expiration of this
Agreement.
3. Compensation and Expenses
● Fees: As consideration for the Services, the Company shall pay the Consultant a
commission rate of twenty percent (20%) of all Gross Revenues from sales directly
generated by the Consultant's introductions, payable monthly.
● Limitation: The Consultant’s right to commission payments is strictly limited to the
Term of this Agreement. The Consultant will not be entitled to any commission payments
for sales originated during the Term if those sales are paid after the termination of this
Agreement.
● Expenses: The Consultant is responsible for all expenses incurred while performing the
Services, except as expressly agreed to in writing by the Company's CEO.
4. Confidentiality and Intellectual Property
● Limited Scope: Given the nature of the Services, the Company will not disclose to the
Consultant any information intended to be "Confidential Information" or "Trade Secrets."
The Company will only provide the Consultant with information that is publicly
disclosed or generally known.
● No IP Creation: The parties acknowledge that the Services are solely referral-based and
do not involve the creation, conception, development, or reduction to practice of any
intellectual property, inventions, or works of authorship, and therefore, no assignment of
Inventions is required by the Consultant under this Agreement.
● Company Property Return: At the time of termination, the Consultant will return to the
Company all devices, records, data, or other property belonging to the Company.
5. Independent Contractor Status and Taxes
● The Consultant shall have full responsibility for all applicable taxes for all
compensation paid under this Agreement.
● The Consultant agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold the Company harmless from any
liability or claims with respect to such taxes or employment requirements.
6. Non-Solicitation
● During the term of the Relationship, and for a period of twelve (12) months
immediately following the termination of the Relationship, the Consultant shall not,
directly or indirectly, solicit any of the Company's employees or consultants to terminate
their relationship with the Company.
7. Indemnification and Liability
● Indemnification: Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other party
from and against all losses, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses arising from any
negligent or wrongful act or omission, or breach of any representation or warranty in this
Agreement by the indemnifying party.
● Limitation of Liability: In no event shall the Company be liable for any indirect,
incidental, special, or consequential damages. The Company's total liability shall not
exceed the amounts paid by the Company to the Consultant under this Agreement for the
Services.
8. Governing Law and Entire Agreement
● Governing Law: The validity and interpretation of this Agreement shall be governed in
accordance with the laws of the state of Delaware.
● Entire Agreement: This Agreement sets forth the entire understanding and supersedes all
prior discussions or agreements relating to the subject matter herein.
